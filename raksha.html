Our Mission

Safety is a right,
not a privilege

Raksha+ is a personal safety app built for real-world emergencies. One tap — your location is shared, your people are alerted, and help is on the way.

🆘
Instant SOS Alerts

One tap sends distress signals to 5 trusted contacts with live GPS coordinates. Works even on slow networks.

📍
Live Location Streaming

Real-time GPS broadcast so your loved ones can track your journey and respond the moment something feels off.

🔒
Privacy by Default

Zero data stored on our servers. Location shared only with contacts you choose, encrypted end-to-end.
